Hardware

Configurable

Each sensor is connected with standard ethernet wires. Space the sensors out equally around your display and calibrate.

Rechargeable

The stylus goes into low power when not in use. Never worry about replacing the batteries, they're rechargeable.

Wrist Armband

The battery pack, radio, and processor for the stylus is part of an armband.

Wireless Stylus

The stylus and wrist armband communicate with the hardware without wires.

Optimized

The emitter/receiver pairs have been optimized for accurately determining position over a standard display size.

Position Tracking

Up to 8 infrared sensors placed around your canvas determine the location of the stylus from the measured intensity of infrared light received by each sensor. Supervised regression is used to train an artificial neural network from these readings.
