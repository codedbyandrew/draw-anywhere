Software

Adjust Color and Weight

Get the hue, brightness, saturation, opacity, and line weight just right with a convenient tooltip. The toolbar and application titlebar dynamically move out of the way while you are drawing on the canvas. You can also rotate the toolbar and move it around the canvas.

Intuitive Keyboard Shortcuts
There's a cheatsheet too.

Just about everything can be toggled or opened from a keyboard shortcut, even the cheatsheet (press ? to bring it up).

Dark Theme
Working late? No problem.

Quickly change between dark and light themes. The settings tooltip also allows you to toggle the grid, line shadow, and change the behavior of the stylus button.

Handwriting Recognition

When you're done writing, copy the plain text version. The application makes use of a local optical character recognition library to determine characters in your canvas after each stroke.

Draw Anywhere.
Literally.

Trace on top of whatever you're working on by toggling the background opacity. Want to add your sketch to a document when you're done? Copy your canvas to the clipboard and paste it or save it to a transparent image.

Artificial Intelligence

Use artificial neural networks to determine the cursor position. First gather calibration data by placing the stylus on indicated locations on the screen. Then train the neural network with the calibration data. Save your calibration data and neural network profiles so you can use it with different styluses and in different locations

View Live Data

Debug signals that you receive over USB live within the main Canvas window.
